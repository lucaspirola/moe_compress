## Description

kdr SHALL compute student-vs-teacher logit distillation loss as forward KL divergence (`KLD(p_teacher || p_student)`) at temperature τ=1.0 with per-token batchmean reduction. Both logit tensors SHALL be promoted to fp32 before softmax to preserve numerical stability under bf16 storage. The teacher MAY be loaded in BF16 (multi-GPU ZeRO-3 default) or FP8 (single-GPU H200/Hopper); in both cases its `lm_head` SHALL be retained at BF16 to preserve logit dynamic range. The teacher's parameters SHALL NOT receive gradients.

## Rationale

This is the Minitron-Chapter-1 logit-only KD recipe (arxiv:2407.14679 §3 Eq.2 + Table 15 ablation; BP#7), reused as the recovery loss for both Ch1 (`bf16` mode) and Ch3 (`da_qad` mode). The fp32-softmax requirement comes from observed bf16 underflow in softmax tails on a 150k-vocab MoE — without it, the loss collapses through low-probability tokens and the gradient signal degrades silently. Teacher freeze is a precondition of distillation; gradient flow into the teacher would couple it to the student and break the soft-target contract.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `forward_kld_loss(student_logits, teacher_logits, temperature=1.0)` reduces to `[B*T, V]` and returns `LogitsDistillationLoss(temperature=T, reduction="batchmean").forward(...)` from modelopt
- [ ] Both `student_logits` and `teacher_logits` are converted to `torch.float32` before softmax
- [ ] Teacher parameters have `requires_grad_=False` after load; a unit test asserts no gradient accumulates on the teacher after a backward pass
- [ ] When the teacher is loaded in FP8, the `lm_head` linear layer is kept at BF16 (verifiable by inspecting the teacher's loaded state_dict dtypes)
- [ ] Vocab-size mismatch between student and teacher logits raises a `ValueError` at function entry with a message naming both sides
