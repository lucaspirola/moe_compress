## Description

When sample generations are enabled, wrap `student.generate(...)` in `with deepspeed.zero.GatheredParameters(student.parameters(), modifier_rank=0):`. Only rank 0 invokes generate; other ranks no-op.

## Rationale

ZeRO-3 sharded params can't be used by `.generate` directly.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] GatheredParameters context covers the entire `.generate` call
- [ ] Other ranks do not invoke `.generate`
