## Description

Implement a `RouterReplayHook` that captures the teacher's MoE expert assignments per microbatch and forces the student's router to produce the same assignments via a forward-time override.

## Rationale

arxiv:2605.05365 §IV-C identifies router replay as the #1 MoE stability technique under precision drift; QAD has even more drift than RL.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A unit test instantiates a small MoE, runs teacher and student forwards, asserts post-hook student expert assignments == teacher's
- [ ] The hook is no-op for non-MoE models
