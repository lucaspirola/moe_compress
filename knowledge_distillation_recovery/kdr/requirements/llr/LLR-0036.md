## Description

`kdr.eval.quick._wikitext2_ppl(student, tokenizer, cfg, accelerator)` iterates the eval data without a distributed sampler, calling `student(input_ids=...)` from every rank with identical input_ids.

## Rationale

Rank-replicated eval is correct under ZeRO-3 collective forward semantics.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] No `DistributedSampler` is constructed
- [ ] Forward is collective (every rank calls it)
